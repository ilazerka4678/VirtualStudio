Here are some of the spots I know in NYC!

  • My favorite place in all of NYC must be Hudson River Park! It has all kinds of parks, restaurants, and is just beautiful, although a little expensive.
  • I also really like an area in Brooklyn called Manhattan Beach - it is really nice to walk through it, as it is quiet and clean.
  • A place I don't like a whole lot is Brighton Beach - it's always loud because of the overhead train and is always covered in trash.
  • A similar place to Brighton Beach is 86th Street and Bay Parkway. Even though that's where I first lived, I definitely don't have fond memories of that area!
